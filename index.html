Our Approach
Early Stage
We invest an initial €500k to €3m, as lead or part of a syndicate, in teams with a working product and early validation with customers
Tech-Enabled
We are looking for disruptive B2B or B2C models that leverage technology (primarily software) to transform the customer experience
European Focus
We back ventures headquartered in Europe or with significant team and commercial focus here
